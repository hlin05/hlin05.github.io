Tao's Multi-scale Electrochemistry Lab Meel

Meel aim to address energy and environmental challenges of our society with electrochemical engineering. Currently, we focus on Li-ion batteries, redox flow batteries and metal anodes. Our approaches are multi-scale: we are not only interested in the materials and interfaces at the atomistic scale, but also the reaction and transport happening at the meso-scale.
